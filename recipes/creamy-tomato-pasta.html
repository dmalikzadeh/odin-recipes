One pot: Creamy tomato pasta

Description

This creamy tomato pasta recipe comes together quickly and uses everyday ingredients. You'll love the silky tomato sauce!

Ingredients:

  • Uncooked penne pasta
  • Red onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato passata
  • Heavy cream
  • Chicken stock cube
  • Shredded mozzerella

Steps:

  1. Heat up a pan with olive oil and cook the onion (chopped) and garlic (minced) until they soften.
  2. Add the tomato passata, chicken stock and heavy cream and mix. Add desired seasonings.
  3. Fill the pan with boiling water and add the uncooked pasta.
  4. Once the pasta is cooked, top with shredded mozzerella and serve.
